We all know that nowadays, Arduino has been a major part of the projects as well as in research activities. Many school and college level projects are now being totally executed with the Arduino as a major ingredient. But, here a question arises, is there any use of these arduino boards or the related projects in real life. Many at times, in the newly updated electronic applications there are arduino boards which are used but in the miniature format. So we can have a closer look at the usage.

We know that Arduino mainly operates on the principle of sensing and actuating so it has a major share in the applications like defence, traffic signal control and medical laboratories. In Defence, it is used for air-traffic control at airports, long range surveillance and early-warning systems in ships. This system is the heart of a missile guidance system. In war a number of small portable radar systems are maintained and operated, as well as systems that occupy several large rooms. When it comes to medical, it is used in hearbeat sensing applications. So like that it is used in many sectors. So with this we can conclude that Arduino is the backbone for this new era of technology.
